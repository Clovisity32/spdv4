# Post-Secondary Admissions Exercise (PSE) Pathways

This document serves as a reference for students to accurately determine their post-secondary pathway eligibility based on subject levels, grade combinations, and aggregate score computations.

---

## 1. Overview of Pathways & Admission Types

| Pathway / Institution                                  | Aggregate Type                      | Admission Criteria Summary                                                                                                      | Application Route                    |
| :----------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :----------------------------------- |
| **Junior College (JC)**                                | L1R4 ≤ 16                           | All subjects used must be taken at G3 level.                                                                                    | Apply in PSE                         |
| **Millennia Institute (MI)**                           | L1R4 ≤ 20                           | All subjects used must be taken at G3 level.                                                                                    | Apply in PSE                         |
| **Polytechnic Diploma**                                | ELR2B2 ≤ 22<br>_(≤ 24 for Nursing)_ | Requires a minimum of 4 subjects at G3 level; only the 5th subject (B2) can be at G2 level.                                     | Apply in PSE                         |
| **Polytechnic Foundation Programme (PFP)**             | ELMAB3 ≤ 12                         | Uses exclusively G2 equivalent grades.                                                                                          | Apply in PSE                         |
| **Arts Institution (NAFA Foundation Programme - NFP)** | Diploma                             | Direct application to school.                                                                                                   | Direct Application                   |
| **ITE (2-Year)**                                       | ELMAB3                              | Direct application to school.                                                                                                   | Direct Application                   |
| **ITE (3-Year)**                                       | R2B2, R1B3, B4                      | Via ITE 3-Year pathway. No maximum aggregate score requirement but must meet subject-specific Minimum Entry Requirements (MER). | Apply in PSE / Internal Acceleration |

### Academic Eligibility Notes:

- **Subject Level Combinations:** General eligibility across the 6 major pathways is determined by specific combinations of subject levels (e.g., 5 G3; 4 G3 + 1 G2; 5 G2; 4 G1).
- **Aggregate Type Computation:** To compute the aggregate type for each pathway, students must take all the required subjects at the specified subject level.
- **ITE 3-Year Accelerated Pathway:** Not available for direct application in PSE. Students taking a 3-Year Higher Nitec course may be offered the accelerated pathway (to attain a Higher Nitec in a shorter duration of about two years) if they meet academic requirements during their Year 1 Semester 1 examinations.

## 2. Explanatory Note on Aggregates Reflected

The aggregates in the Holistic Development Profile are intended to provide a broad measure of current academic performance, and **should not be interpreted to determine eventual eligibility for different post-secondary options**.

### Aggregate Composition Table

| Aggregate(s) | Aggregate Composition                                                                                                                                                 |
| :----------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **L1R4**     | **L1:** G3 English Language or G3 Higher Mother Tongue Language<br>**R4:** 4 relevant G3 subjects                                                                     |
| **ELB4**     | **EL:** G3 English Language<br>**B4:** 3 best G3 and 1 best G2 subjects _(G3 grade would be mapped to equivalent G2 grade)_                                           |
| **ELMAB3**   | **EL:** G2 English Language<br>**MA:** G2 Mathematics / G2 Additional Mathematics<br>**B3:** 3 best G2 subjects _(G3 grades would be mapped to equivalent G2 grades)_ |
| **B4**       | **B4:** 4 best G1 subjects _(G3 and G2 grades would be mapped to equivalent G1 grades)_                                                                               |

## 3. Downward Mapping of Grades

For students taking subjects at more demanding levels, downward grade mapping is used to compute the respective aggregate scores.

| G3 Subject Grade | G2 Subject Grade | G1 Subject Grade |
| :--------------: | :--------------: | :--------------: |
|    A1, A2, B3    |        1         |        A         |
|    B4, C5, C6    |        2         |                  |
|        D7        |        3         |                  |
|        E8        |        4         |        B         |
|        9         |        5         |        C         |
|                  |        6         |        D         |
|                  |                  |        E         |

---

## 4. Invalid Grade Exceptions

- **For Polytechnic ELR2B2 and PFP ELMAB3:** G3 grade 9 and G2 grades 5 & 6 **cannot** be used for computation.
- **For ITE 2-Year ELMAB3:** G3 grade 9 and G2 grade 6 **can** be used for aggregate computation, but **cannot** fulfil subject-specific Minimum Entry Requirements (MER).
- **For ITE 3-Year R2B2/R1B3/B4:** G3 grade 9, G2 grade 6, and G1 Grade E **can** be used for aggregate computation, but **cannot** fulfil subject-specific Minimum Entry Requirements (MER).

## 5. Pathway-Specific Details & Aggregate Computations

### A. G1 – ITE Pathway (ITE 3-Year Higher Nitec)

- **Aggregate Types:** `[R2B2]`, `[R1B3]`, or `[B4]`
- **Score Requirement:** No maximum aggregate score requirement, but must meet the subject-specific Minimum Entry Requirement (MER) of the aggregate types (except `[B4]`, which has no MER).
- **Course Selection:** All courses within the same aggregate type share the same MER. If you meet the MER for the aggregate type, you will be eligible to apply for courses under that aggregate type.
- **Grade Conversion:** G3, G2, and G1 grades will be converted to ITE aggregate points to compute the aggregate scores.
- **Grade Requirement based on Subject Level:** G3 &rarr; A1-E8, G2 &rarr; 1-5, and G1 &rarr; A-D.

#### ITE 3-Year Aggregate Type Formulations:

- **`[R2B2]`:** `[R1: English]` + `[R2: Math]` + `[B1]` + `[B2]`
  - _Grade requirements apply for R1, R2, B1. No grade requirement for B2._
- **`[R1B3-A]`:** `[R1: Math]` + `[B1]` + `[B2]` + `[B3]`
  - _Grade requirements apply for R1, B1, B2. No grade requirement for B3._
- **`[R1B3-B]`:** `[R1: Math or Science]` + `[B1]` + `[B2]` + `[B3]`
- **`[R1B3-C]`:** `[R1: English]` + `[B1]` + `[B2]` + `[B3]`
- **`[B4]`:** `[B1]` + `[B2]` + `[B3]` + `[B4]`
  - _No grade requirement._

### B. G2 – PFP Pathway (Polytechnic Foundation Programme)

- **Aggregate Type:** ELMAB3 (1-Year Prep route to Specific Diploma Courses or Broad Clusters; subject to changes in 2028).
- **Score Requirement:** Gross aggregate score must not exceed **12**.
- **Formula:** `[EL: English]` + `[MA: Math]` + `[B1: Best Subject]` + `[B2: Best Subject]` + `[B3: Best Subject]`
- **Rules:**
  - Uses exclusively G2 equivalent grades. G3 grades are mapped to equivalent G2 grades.
  - G3 grade 9 and G2 grades 5 or 6 **cannot** be used for ELMAB3 aggregate score computation.
  - Must meet the Minimum Entry Requirement (MER) of the cluster/courses that you are applying for.

---

### C. G3 – Polytechnic Pathway (Polytechnic Diploma)

- **Aggregate Type:** ELR2B2 (Four Aggregate Types: ELR2B2-A/B/C/D).
- **Score Requirement:** NET aggregate score must not exceed **22** _(or 24 for Nursing courses)_. Must meet the course-specific Minimum Entry Requirement (MER).
- **Formula:** `[EL]` + `[R1]` + `[R2]` + `[B1]` + `[B2]`
  - `[EL]`, `[R1]`, `[R2]`, `[B1]` must be taken at G3 subject level (computed using G3 grade).
  - `[B2]` can be taken at G2 or G3 subject level (computed using G2 equivalent grade).
- **Scenario (Using two G3 subjects for B1 and B2):** The better G3 grade will be used as `[B1]`. The other G3 grade maps to its G2 equivalent grade to be used as `[B2]`.
- **Exceptions:** G3 grade 9 and G2 grades 5 or 6 **cannot** be used for ELR2B2 aggregate score computation.
- **CCA Bonus Points:** Deducted from the Gross aggregate score to compute the Net aggregate score:
  - **Excellent (A1/A2):** 2 points
  - **Good (B3 – C6):** 1 point

---

### D. G3 – JC / MI Pathway (Junior Colleges & Millennia Institute)

- **Aggregate Type:** L1R4
- **Score Requirements:**
  - **Junior College (JC) Admission:** Gross Aggregate must be **≤ 16**.
  - **Millennia Institute (MI) Admission:** Gross Aggregate must be **≤ 20**.
- **Formula:** `[L1]` + `[R1]` + `[R2]` + `[R3]` + `[R4]`
  - `[L1]` : G3 English or HMTL
  - `[R1]` : Best G3 Humanities
  - `[R2]` : Best G3 Math or Science
  - `[R3]` : Best G3 Humanities, Math or Science
  - `[R4]` : Any best G3 subjects
- **Rules:**
  - All subjects used in aggregate score computation for JC and MI admissions must be taken at G3.
  - Mother Tongue Language (MTL) and Higher MTL (HMTL) **cannot** be used in the same aggregate computation.
  - Religious Knowledge **cannot** be used.

#### Minimum Subject Grade Requirements:

- **G3 English Language:** A1 to C6
- **Any 1 Mathematics (G3 Math or G3 A-Math):** A1 to D7
- **Any 1 Mother Tongue Language (MTL) or MTL-in-lieu\*:** G3 (A1-D7) / G2 (1-5) / G1 (A-D) **[OR]** Higher MTL: G3 (A1 – E8)
  - _\*MTL-in-lieu subjects include: Arabic, Bengali, Burmese, French, German, Gujarati, Hindi, Japanese, Panjabi, Thai, Urdu._
- **Absent Grade Disqualification:** Students receiving an Absent grade for any of the required subjects are ineligible for conditional admission.

#### Conditional Admission (CA) Criteria:

Students who did not meet the minimum grade for English, Mathematics, or Mother Tongue are not automatically disqualified if they meet the following specific criteria:

- **Conditional Admission to a JC:**
  - Your L1R4 gross aggregate score is **≤ 12** **[OR]**
  - Your L1R4 gross aggregate score is between **13 to 16** and you have grades of A1 or A2 in all your 4 relevant subjects.
  - _Requirement:_ Admitted CA students must re-sit the specific subject and obtain the required grade during their JC Year 1. If they fail to pass by the end of Year 1, the JC student must transfer to MI Year 2.

- **Conditional Admission to an MI:**
  - Your L1R4 gross aggregate score is **≤ 15** **[OR]**
  - Your L1R4 gross aggregate score is between **16 to 20** and you have grades of A1 or A2 in all your 4 relevant subjects.
  - _Requirement:_ CA students must re-sit the specific subject and obtain the required grade by the MI Year 2 deadline. If they fail to pass by the end of Year 1, they continue in MI Year 2. However, students who fail to obtain the required grade by the final Year 2 deadline will be asked to leave the institution.
